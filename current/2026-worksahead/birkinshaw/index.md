Our annual sample of new performance returns to <a href="https://contactmcr.com/events/works-ahead-2026" target="_blank">Contact</a> with a new work-in-development from Nathan Birkinshaw…         
         
*"Making peace with the fact this might be it."*         
         
An exploration of contentment, led by a man brimming with mediocrity.         
         
Through film, performance, song, and an increasingly questionable process, comes a show that investigates how to find meaning in the mundane — that somehow becomes increasingly more performative itself as time slips by.         
         
Please note: Nathan makes a guest appearance in [Social Experiment](/socialexperiment) to show *How To Be Content*.

#### Artist         
Nathan is a performance artist, comedian, and theatre-maker whose work blends humour with the explorations of being human and the general absurdity of what comes with that responsibility (or lack thereof).<br>With an unhealthy obsession with the internet, you're just as likely to find him there as well as showing work in theatres, back rooms of pubs, and having work appearing on BBC Radio.         
         
#### About         
Each year since 2012, [Works Ahead](/hab/worksahead) has invited artists to dig deeper into work presented at the previous year’s [Emergency](/hab/emergency) or other platform event.          
        
A project developed out of the old Method Lab & SeedFund schemes which [hÅb](/hab) ran with greenroom until 2011; [Works Ahead](/hab/worksahead) has offered 39 small, supported, work-in-development commissions for artists over the past fifteen years — often in partnership with <a href="https://contactmcr.com" target="_blank">Contact</a>.
